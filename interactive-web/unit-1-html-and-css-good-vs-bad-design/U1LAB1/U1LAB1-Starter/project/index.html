This is Paramore.

Paramore is a rock band that's most known by their songs 'Still Into You', 'All I Wanted', and 'The Only Exception'. Paramore was formed in 2004. Originally, it consisted of four members: Josh Farro (who played guitar), Zac Farro (who plays drums), Jeremy Davis (who played bass guitar), and Hayley Williams (lead vocals). Today, it consists of only three members, two of which were part of the original formation of the band, Hayley Williams and Zac Farro. The third member, Taylor York, plays guitar.

Discography:

  • All We Know is Falling (2008)
  • Riot! (2010)
  • Brand New Eyes (2010)
  • Paramore (2013)
  • After Laughter (2017)
  • This Is Why (2023)

Awards:

Paramore has been nominated for 107 awards, and won 31 of them including:

  • Best Rock Album 2024
  • Best Rock Song 2015
  • Best Vocalist 2015
  • Favorite Rock Band 2010 & 2011

Paramore also recorded songs for the Twilight movie, 'Decode' and 'I Caught Myself' in 2008.
